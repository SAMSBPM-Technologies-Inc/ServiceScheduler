# Customer Guide

This guide explains how to subscribe to a service, manage your subscription, and make payments on ServStack.

---

## Finding Your Vendor's Portal

Your service provider will share a link to their portal. It looks like:

```
https://servstack.ca/portal/<vendor-name>
```

or their own custom domain, e.g.:

```
https://subscribe.freshmeals.com
```

All your interactions happen through that link — you are dealing with your service provider's branded portal.

---

## Creating an Account

1. Visit your vendor's portal link
2. Click **Register** (or **Sign Up**)
3. Enter your name, email address, and a password
4. Click **Create Account**

You are logged in immediately after registration.

---

## Browsing Plans

The portal homepage shows all active plans offered by your service provider.

Each plan card shows:
- The plan name and description
- The plan type (fixed tiers or build-your-own)
- Pricing information

Click **View Plan** or **Subscribe** to see the full details.

---

## Subscribing to a Fixed Plan

A Fixed Plan has preset tiers and product choices.

1. Click **Subscribe** on the plan
2. Choose a **schedule tier** (e.g. Weekly at $49, Monthly at $179)
3. For each product group, make your selection:
   - If the rule is **"Choose 1"** — pick one option from the list
   - If the rule is **"Choose N"** — pick exactly N options (a counter shows how many remain)
   - If the group is **"All included"** — no action needed, all items are always included
4. Review your choices
5. Click **Subscribe & Pay**
6. You are redirected to Stripe Checkout to complete payment
7. After payment, your subscription is **Active**

---

## Subscribing to a Configurable Plan

A Configurable Plan lets you build your own custom schedule.

1. Click **Subscribe** on the plan
2. Check the boxes next to the services you want
3. For each selected service, choose how often you want it:
   - Daily, Weekly, or Monthly (only the options your provider has set up are shown)
4. A running total is shown as you make selections
5. Click **Subscribe & Pay**
6. You are redirected to Stripe Checkout to complete payment
7. After payment, your subscription is **Active**

---

## Managing Your Subscription

After subscribing, go to **My Subscription** from the portal menu.

### Viewing Your Subscription

You can see:
- Your current plan and status
- Your selected products or service schedule
- Any special instructions on file
- Your payment history

### Editing Your Selections

If your service provider allows changes, you can update what you receive:

- **Fixed plan:** Click **Edit Selections** to change your product choices within the allowed rules
- **Configurable plan:** Click **Edit Schedule** to add, remove, or change the frequency of services

Changes take effect from the next service period.

### Pausing Your Subscription

If you need to temporarily stop your service, contact your service provider to pause your subscription. Paused subscriptions retain all your settings and can be resumed at any time.

### Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel your subscription, contact your service provider. Once cancelled, the subscription cannot be reactivated — you would need to subscribe again.

---

## Payments

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. ServStack never stores your card details.

### How Billing Works

- When you subscribe, you complete the first payment via Stripe Checkout
- Subsequent billing cycles generate a new payment record
- You will be directed to Stripe to pay each cycle

### Payment History

Go to **My Subscription → Payments** to see all your payment records:
- Amount and billing period
- Status (Pending / Paid / Failed / Refunded)
- Date of payment

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails, the status will show as **Failed**. Contact your service provider to resolve the issue and retry payment.

---

## Instructions and Notes

You can add notes to your subscription — useful for delivery instructions, access codes, or preferences.

1. Go to **My Subscription**
2. Click **Add Instructions** or **Edit Instructions**
3. Type your note and save

Your service provider's team will see these notes when managing your subscription.

---

## Logging Out

Click your name in the top right corner and select **Sign Out**.

---

## Getting Help

If you have questions about your subscription, contact your service provider directly. ServStack is the platform that powers their portal, not the service provider itself.
